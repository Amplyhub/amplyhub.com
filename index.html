The App

Three screens.
Endless possibilities.

Amply is built around three purpose-built pages — each one crafted for a distinct part of your audio experience.

Screen 01

Home — Listen

Your personal audio feed. Stream music, discover new tracks, and earn Plyz Tokens automatically as audio ads play between songs.

Screen 02

Discover — Explore

Search for artists, brands and pages. Browse curated playlists, find affiliated partners and connect with creators you care about.

Screen 03

My Plyz

Your rewards hub and profile in one. Access the offerwall, complete surveys, track your Plyz balance, manage settings and cash out.

Audio Promotion

Run targeted audio campaigns that put your sound directly in listeners' ears. Precise targeting by genre, city, age and listening behaviour.
